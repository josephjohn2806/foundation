Who We Are

Stephen & Grace Safe Haven Foundation is a community-based organization dedicated to supporting orphans, vulnerable families, and children at risk. Through education, shelter, counseling, and spiritual nurturing, we work to build brighter futures and restore dignity to every life we touch.
