What We Do

“Restoring Hope, Protecting Every Child.”

Our Core Programs

1. Safe Shelter & Protection

A secure home environment for children who have faced abuse. We offer 24-hour protection, meals, and psychosocial support.

2. Trauma Counseling

Professional therapy sessions that help survivors process trauma, rebuild confidence, and recover emotionally.

3. Education & Skills

Ensuring every child returns to education or acquires vocational skills suited to their unique talents and interests.

4. Empowerment Programs

Life skills, leadership, and health awareness training for boys and girls to break the silence around assault.

5. Community Advocacy

Raising awareness about child abuse and early marriage while strengthening families through parenting workshops.

6. Sustainability Projects

Agribusiness and tailoring workshops that sustain the Foundation and provide employment for our older youth.

Our Holistic Approach

Every child who joins our care receives a personalized recovery plan focusing on health, education, and belonging.

  • Trauma-informed counseling
  • Access to safe learning environments
  • Family reintegration support
  • Ongoing mentorship and stability

Governance & Sustainability

The Foundation is guided by a Board of Trustees including child protection specialists and educators. Our model includes donor partnerships and income projects like farming and eco-products to ensure long-term care for our children.
