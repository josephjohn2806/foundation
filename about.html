About Us

The Stephen & Grace Safe Haven Foundation is a child-focused non-profit organization based in Arusha, Tanzania. We exist to protect, heal, and empower children who have survived assault, neglect, or unsafe environments.

Our Vision

To create a Tanzania where every child lives free from fear, protected from harm, and empowered to thrive in dignity and hope.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive care, safety, and empowerment for children living in dangerous environments — particularly survivors of assault — through protection, counseling, education, and community engagement.

Our Story

The Stephen & Grace Safe Haven Foundation was founded out of compassion and lived experience. It began as a small community effort to support vulnerable children in Arusha and grew into a structured organization recognized for its trauma-informed, survivor-centered approach.

Over the years,we have worked with local authorities,social welfare offices,schools,and family to identify children in danger,rescue them from abusive situations, and help them rebuld their lives. Our name represents faith and humanity-a belief that no matter how dark a child's beginning may be, they can still grow into light and possibility.
