About Us

Our Heart, Our Mission, Our Commitment

The Stephen Haven Foundation is a child-focused non-profit based in Arusha, Tanzania. We protect, heal, and empower children who have survived assault and neglect.

Our Vision

To create a Tanzania where every child lives free from fear and is empowered to thrive in dignity and love.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive care, safety, and empowerment through professional counseling and quality education.

Core Values

  • Compassion
  • Integrity
  • Respect
  • Empowerment
  • Equality
  • Community

Our Story

The Stephen Haven Foundation was founded out of compassion and lived experience. It began as a small community effort to support vulnerable children in Arusha and grew into a structured organization recognized for its trauma-informed approach.
