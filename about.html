About Us

Stephen Safe Haven Foundation

The Stephen Safe Haven Foundation is a child-focused non-profit based in Arusha, Tanzania. We protect, heal, and empower children who have survived assault and neglect.

Our Vision

To create a Tanzania where every child lives free from fear and empowered to thrive in dignity.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive care, safety, and empowerment through counseling and education.

Core Values

  • Compassion
  • Integrity
  • Respect
  • Empowerment
  • Equality
  • Community

Our Story

The Stephen Safe Haven Foundation was founded out of compassion and lived experience. It began as a small community effort to support vulnerable children in Arusha and grew into a structured organization recognized for its trauma-informed approach.
