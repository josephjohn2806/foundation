The Origin

Two Pundous, One Vision

JB Cleaning Company began not just as a business venture, but as a journey of true brotherhood. Founded by Joseph John and Peter Regan, the roots of our values trace back to the serene, rolling landscapes of Kyulu.

It was there in Kyulu that these two pundous first met. Sharing a profound appreciation for discipline, aesthetic beauty, and meticulous attention to detail, their instant connection transcended mere friendship—they became family.

By combining their shared values and dedication to precision, they established JB Cleaning Company. Today, that legacy of trust, family bond, and uncompromising premium service is infused into every pristine space we care for.
